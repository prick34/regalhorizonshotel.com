    Introduction

    Be a part of the Regal Horizons family, where hospitality meets excellence.

    Why Work With Us?

    At Regal Horizons Hotel, we believe in creating a welcoming and supportive environment for our team. Here’s why you’ll love working with us:

    • Competitive salaries and benefits
    • Opportunities for career growth
    • Training and development programs
    • A supportive and inclusive work culture
    • Employee discounts on hotel stays and services

    Available Positions

    We are currently hiring for the following roles:

    • Front Desk Associate: Deliver exceptional customer service and assist with guest check-ins and check-outs.
    • Housekeeping Staff: Maintain clean and comfortable guest rooms and common areas.
    • Restaurant Server: Provide excellent dining experiences to our guests.
    • Marketing Specialist: Promote our brand and manage social media campaigns.
    • Event Coordinator: Plan and execute memorable events and meetings.

    How to Apply

    We’re excited to hear from you! To apply for any of the positions listed, please follow these steps:

    • Prepare your resume and cover letter.
    • Email your application to careers@regalhorizons.com.
    • Include the position title in the subject line of your email.
