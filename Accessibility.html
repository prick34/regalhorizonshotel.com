    Accessibility Commitment

    At Regal Horizons Hotel, we are dedicated to providing an inclusive experience for all our guests.

    Our Commitment

    We aim to ensure that every guest, including those with disabilities, has a comfortable and enjoyable stay. Our facilities and services are designed to provide seamless access for all.

    Accessible Features

    • Wheelchair-accessible entrances and elevators
    • Accessible parking spaces near the main entrances
    • Rooms equipped with roll-in showers and grab bars
    • Accessible public restrooms
    • Visual and auditory aids upon request
    • Service animal-friendly policy

    Website Accessibility

    We have optimized our website to meet Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1. This includes:

    • Keyboard navigation support
    • Alt text for images
    • Screen reader compatibility
    • High-contrast modes

    Need Assistance?

    If you have any questions or need additional assistance, our team is available 24/7. Please contact us via:

    • Email: accessibility@regalhorizons.com
    • Phone: +1-800-555-6789
    • In-person assistance at the front desk

    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is your return policy?

    Our return policy allows returns within 30 days of purchase with a valid receipt.

    How do I track my order?

    You can track your order using the tracking link sent to your email after purchase.

    Do you offer international shipping?

    Yes, we ship internationally. Shipping fees and times vary by location.
