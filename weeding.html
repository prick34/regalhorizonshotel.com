    AN OCEAN VIEW AISLE

    The Terrace

    Overlooking Falmouth Bay with panoramic views across the verdant grounds, the terrace is a bright and scenic setting for your special day. Glass doors lead to our suntrap patio, manicured gardens, and palm-tree lined lawns.

    This option offers up to 100 guests for your civil ceremony and evening reception, and can accommodate 60 for your wedding breakfast.

    A SEAT BY THE SEA

    The Lido

    Enjoy your own private portion of our grand dining area, with the most stunning seaside scenery and views of our green lawns and tropical gardens as the perfect backdrop to your day.

    With plenty of natural light drifting in through expansive windows, you and your guests can make the most of a magic ocean-side moment.

    Ceremony and receptions

    The Pendennis Ballroom

    A nod to Falmouth’s castle just around the coastal path, The Pendennis Ballroom is an elegant choice for accommodating up to 100 guests for a civil ceremony and wedding breakfast, and 160 guests for evening receptions.

    Intricate ceiling details and Victorian architecture are married with stunning views across the lawns, exclusive use of a private bar, and a dancefloor. This space in our beachside wedding venue in Cornwall is perfect for the event with all the frills.
