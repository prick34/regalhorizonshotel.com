    Aaron's Easy Mince Pie Recipe

    Mince pies are a holiday classic, and with this simple recipe, you can whip up a batch of these delicious treats in no time! Whether you're a seasoned baker or a beginner, Aaron's easy mince pie recipe ensures you’ll impress your family and friends.

    Ingredients You'll Need

    • 1 jar (400g) of mincemeat
    • 250g plain flour
    • 125g unsalted butter (cold, cubed)
    • 2-3 tbsp cold water

    Step-by-Step Instructions

    Step 1: Make the Pastry

    • In a large bowl, combine the flour and cubed butter. Rub them together with your fingertips until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs.
    • Add the icing sugar and mix.
    • Stir in the egg yolk and cold water gradually, mixing until the dough comes together.
    • Wrap the dough in cling film and chill for 20 minutes.

    Step 2: Roll and Cut

    Preheat your oven to 200°C (180°C fan).

    On a lightly floured surface, roll out the pastry to about 3mm thick.

    Use a round cutter to cut out 12 bases and slightly smaller stars or circles for the tops.
