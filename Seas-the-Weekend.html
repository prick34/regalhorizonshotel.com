    Seas the Weekend

    Escape the hustle of everyday life and immerse yourself in a luxurious seaside retreat at Regal Horizon Hotel. With stunning ocean views, world-class amenities, and activities designed to rejuvenate your mind and body, your perfect weekend getaway is just a booking away.

    Your Perfect Weekend Itinerary

    Afternoon: Check in and be greeted with a complimentary welcome drink. Settle into your room or suite, designed with comfort and style in mind, all offering spectacular ocean views.

    Evening: Kick off the weekend with dinner at Celestial Bites, our fine-dining restaurant, where you can enjoy fresh seafood specialties paired with expertly selected wines. End the evening stargazing on the Sky Terrace with a handcrafted cocktail.

    Saturday: Explore and Unwind

    Morning: Start your day with a sunrise yoga session on the terrace, followed by a hearty breakfast at Sunrise Caf√©, featuring a buffet of fresh fruits, pastries, and hot dishes.

    Afternoon: Choose your adventure: Take a guided paddleboarding tour to explore the coastline. Relax poolside with a tropical drink in hand. Visit our luxurious Horizon Wellness Spa for a signature massage or facial.

    Evening: Enjoy a themed Coastal Feast Buffet with live music. Let the melodies and the sea breeze set the perfect mood for the night.
