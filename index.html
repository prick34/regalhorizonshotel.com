    What facilities does Regal Horizons Hotel offer?

    Guests at NEW ZEALAND can take advantage of a range of facilities, including complimentary WiFi, a peaceful bar, an inviting restaurant, and a breakfast service. Additionally, for those keen to explore the outdoors, we offer bike rental services. Discover more about our hotel facilities..

    are you hiring Foreign Workers?

    Yes, we are hiring foreign workers for different positions in the hotel. send your application to our email address at info@regalhorizonshotel.com

    Does Regal Horizons Hotel offer breakfast, lunch, and dinner?

    Yes, we serve a continental breakfast buffet every morning, featuring a selection of yogurts, cereals, breads, cooked items, fresh juice, as well as tea and coffee. Our lobby, open 24/7 offers a variety of snacks and pizzas and our restaurant is open for dinner each evening serving a รก la carte menu.

    How close is Best Regal Horizons Hotel?

    For business visitors, Regal Horizons Hotel is perfectly situated near major venues like the World Fashion Centre and Nelson, offering convenience and ease of access. Airport is also easily accessible from our location.

    How do I change or cancel a reservation?

    Changing or cancelling your reservation depends on the type of reservation you have made. If you have booked via our own website and with a flexible rate, you can cancel and/or change your stay free of charge up to 24 hours (until 3:00 PM) before your arrival date. However, this does not apply to non-refundable rates and bookings not made directly with the hotel. For the latter bookings, please contact the company where you made the reservation.

    How can I change my reservation?

    In many cases this can be done directly online via the website of the party where you made your reservation. If the reservation was made directly with the hotel or via the hotel website, you can contact the hotel.

    What is the policy regarding children??

    Children up to 3 years free. Maximum of two children per room per two paying adults in existing bedding. From the age of 4, a child must have their own bed. Meal costs may apply.

    Can I make reservations for a group?

    We apply our group conditions from 5 rooms. If you wish to book a group, please contact us directly for the options.
