    Coast to Coast Charity Dinner at our sister hotel, The Alverton

    At Regal Horizon Hotel, we are proud to support our sister property, The Alverton, as they host the highly anticipated Coast to Coast Charity Dinner. This exclusive event promises an unforgettable evening of fine dining, community spirit, and philanthropy, all in support of an incredible cause.

    A Culinary Journey from Coast to Coast

    Guests will embark on a culinary journey inspired by the best of local produce. The talented chefs at The Alverton have crafted a multi-course menu celebrating the flavors of the coastlines, with dishes that showcase fresh seafood, seasonal vegetables, and artisanal delights. Each course will be paired with fine wines, carefully selected to complement the flavors of the meal. From the first bite to the last, you’ll savor the creativity and passion that goes into every dish.
