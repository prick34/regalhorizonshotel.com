    Introduction

    Information We Collect

    We collect personal data to provide and improve our services, including but not limited to:

    • Your name, contact details, and payment information when booking a room
    • Preferences shared during your stay
    • Technical data such as IP address and device information when using our website

    How We Use Your Information

    We use your information to:

    • Facilitate reservations and provide customer service
    • Enhance your experience with personalized services
    • Comply with legal obligations
    • Send promotional offers and updates (with your consent)

    Sharing of Information

    We respect your privacy and do not sell your personal data. However, we may share it with:

    • Service providers who assist with hotel operations
    • Authorities to comply with legal requirements
    • Marketing partners (only with your explicit consent)

    Data Security

    We implement robust measures to protect your data, including encryption and secure servers. While we strive to safeguard your information, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

    Your Rights

    You have rights concerning your personal data, including:

    • Accessing and reviewing your data
    • Requesting corrections or deletions
    • Withdrawing consent for marketing communications
